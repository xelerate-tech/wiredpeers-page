1\. Signing in
--------------

1.  Open the app in your browser.
2.  Click **Sign in with Google** and pick your work account.
3.  You land on the **Dashboard**.

Use the same browser profile you use for work email so sign-in stays effortless.

2\. Getting around
------------------

The left sidebar lists every section you can access. Your profile and sign-out are in the top right. Most pages have a search box and filters at the top, a list/table in the middle, and a primary action button (e.g. **Create**) in the top-right corner.

3\. Dashboard
-------------

The dashboard is your home base. It shows quick-look cards for:

*   **Pending feedback** — feedback you owe to someone.
*   **Completed feedback** — what you've already submitted this cycle.
*   **Pending reimbursements** — expenses still being reviewed.

Click any card to jump straight to the relevant section.

4\. Goals
---------

Goals let you record what you're working towards and track progress over a cycle.

### Create a goal

1.  Open **Goals** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **New Goal**.
3.  Fill in the title, description, and target date.
4.  Save. The goal appears in your list.

### Update a goal

*   Open the goal and edit progress, status (e.g. In Progress / Done), or notes.
*   Your manager can see your goals — keep them up to date.

5\. Feedback
------------

Feedback is the core of the app. You will both **give** feedback and **receive** it.

### Giving feedback you've been asked for

1.  Open **Feedback** → **Pending**.
2.  Pick a request from the list to open the form.
3.  Answer the questions. You can **Save Draft** at any time and come back later.
4.  When ready, click **Submit**.

Feedback for Alex Rivera Cycle: Q2 2026 · Peer review 1\. What does Alex do well? Excellent communicator, keeps the team aligned on priorities… 2\. One area to improve Save draft Submit

A feedback form — use “Save draft” until you're ready to submit.

Once submitted, you cannot edit your answers. Use Save Draft until you're sure.

### Requesting peer feedback about yourself

1.  Open **Feedback** → **Request Peer Feedback**.
2.  Search and select up to the allowed number of peers.
3.  Choose the form (if more than one is available).
4.  Send the request — your peers will be notified.

### Viewing feedback you've received

*   Open **Feedback** → **About me**.
*   You'll see feedback grouped by cycle. Open any entry to read the full answers.

### Visibility

By default, peer feedback about you is anonymous to you. Some entries may be set as visible — those will show the author's name.

6\. Performance & History
-------------------------

The **History** section is your personal archive across past review cycles.

*   Use the cycle dropdown to switch between cycles.
*   The **All / Received / Given** tabs filter feedback for the selected cycle.
*   Goals tied to a cycle are listed beside their feedback for context.

7\. Expenses
------------

Submit expenses you want reimbursed, and track their status.

### Submit an expense

Expenses always belong to an **event** (a trip, project, off-site, etc.). Your manager or an admin creates the event first; you then add your expenses to it.

1.  Open **Expenses** in the sidebar — the **Events** tab lists every event you can submit against.
2.  Click the **event** your expense belongs to.
3.  On the event page, click **Submit Expense**.
4.  Pick the **category**, enter the **amount**, and add a short description.
5.  Attach a receipt — drag a file in or use the camera button on mobile.
6.  Click **Submit**.

Don't see the event you need? Ask your manager to create it — only managers and admins can open new events.

Submit expense · Client visit — Lisbon, May 2026 Category Transport Amount €42.50 Date 15 May 2026 Description Train ticket to client office Drag receipt here or use 📷 camera Submit

The submit-expense form opens from inside an event, so the event is already set — you just fill in the details and attach a receipt.

### Portuguese invoice QR — auto-fill & NIF check

When you **take a photo** of the receipt (or upload an image of it), the app looks for the QR code printed on Portuguese invoices ("Fatura"). If it finds one, it reads it before doing anything else and:

*   Fills in the fields it can read from the QR — the **date** of the invoice and the **amount**.
*   Compares the **buyer NIF** on the invoice with the **NIF of the company linked to the event**. If they don't match, a red warning appears (e.g. _"Invoice NIF (123456789) does not match event company NIF (987654321)"_) and the **Submit Expense** button is blocked until you fix it.

If the NIF warning appears, it usually means the vendor billed the wrong company (or your personal NIF). Ask for a corrected invoice issued to the company's NIF shown in the warning.

Auto-fill is just a convenience — you can always edit any field the QR pre-filled before submitting. PDFs are not scanned (only photos and image uploads), and non-Portuguese QR codes are ignored.

### Track your expenses

*   The **My Expenses** tab shows the status of each: _Submitted_, _Approved_, _Rejected_, or _Reimbursed_.
*   Click a row to see the receipt and any reviewer notes.

If an expense is rejected you'll usually find the reason in the comments — fix the issue and submit a new one.

8\. Tickets
-----------

Tickets are how you ask other teams (IT, People Team, Facilities, etc.) for help.

### Create a ticket

1.  Open **Tickets** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **Create Ticket**.
3.  Pick the **team** that should handle it.
4.  Write a clear **title** and **description**.
5.  Attach screenshots or files if relevant.
6.  Click **Submit**.

### Track your tickets

*   The **My Tickets** view lists every ticket you've filed.
*   The cards at the top show **My Open Tickets** and **Total Submitted**. Click a card to filter the list to that group.
*   Use the status filter (checkbox dropdown) to combine more than one status, e.g. _In Progress_ + _Waiting on you_.

### Inside a ticket

*   You can add **comments** to give more detail or answer questions from the team handling it.
*   You can add more **attachments** at any time.
*   You'll get email notifications when the status changes, a comment is added, or it is assigned to someone.

9\. Tips & troubleshooting
--------------------------

*   **Lost your work?** Long feedback forms support _Save Draft_ — use it often.
*   **Not seeing something you expect?** Refresh the page; the app updates many lists automatically but a refresh resets everything.
*   **Can't sign in?** Make sure you're using your work Google account and that your admin has added you to the system.
*   **Need help?** File a ticket to the IT or People team from the **Tickets** module.
