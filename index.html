Everything your team needs, nothing they don't

Five tightly integrated modules that cover the full employee and manager workflow — from setting goals to getting reimbursed.

🎯

Goals & Review Cycles

Employees set goals with titles, descriptions, and target dates. Managers see every report's goals per cycle and can track progress toward each one — keeping the whole team aligned.

💬

360° Feedback

Run structured peer reviews, self-assessments, and manager evaluations. Drafts save automatically so nothing is lost. Visibility rules let managers decide what feedback each employee sees.

💸

Expense Management

Employees submit expenses against manager-created events (trips, off-sites, client visits). Attach a receipt, hit Submit — your manager gets notified immediately and can approve or reject with a reason.

🧾

Smart Receipt Scanning

Take a photo of a Portuguese invoice and the app reads the QR code automatically — pre-filling the date and amount. It also checks that the buyer NIF on the invoice matches the company, blocking mismatched submissions before they reach your manager.

🎫

Internal Help Desk

Raise tickets to IT, People Ops, or Facilities with a title, description, priority, and attachments. Agents claim tickets, move them through a configurable workflow, and reply in a comment thread — you get notified at every step.

👥

Team Dashboard

Managers get a My Team view in list or org-tree format showing each report's goals, feedback received, and self-review status for the selected cycle. Drill into any report to write a manager review, send peer-feedback requests, or give 1:1 feedback.

One platform, two roles

WiredPeers adapts to how you work — whether you're an individual contributor or running a team.

For Employees

Your day-to-day toolkit

Everything you need to stay on track, give great feedback, get reimbursed, and get help fast — all in one place.

  • Set and update personal goals each review cycle
  • Respond to peer-feedback requests with save-draft support
  • Request peer reviews about yourself with a few clicks
  • Submit expenses against events and track them from submitted to reimbursed
  • Auto-fill expense fields by photographing a Portuguese invoice QR code
  • Raise tickets to IT, People Ops, or Facilities and follow progress in real time
  • View your feedback history across all past review cycles
For Managers

Your team control room

Everything employees have, plus the tools to approve spend, run reviews, and support your reports through every cycle.

  • Approve or reject team expenses with required rejection reasons
  • Create expense events (trips, off-sites) for the team to submit against
  • View every report's goals, feedback, and self-review status per cycle
  • Browse the org tree to understand direct and indirect report structure
  • Initiate manager reviews, send peer-feedback requests, and give 1:1 feedback
  • Work tickets as an agent if assigned to a ticket team by an admin
  • Sidebar badge keeps pending approvals always visible

Up and running in minutes

From first login to a completed review cycle — no setup headaches.

1

Sign in with Google

Open WiredPeers in your browser and click Sign in with Google. Use your work account and you land straight on the Dashboard — no passwords, no setup.

2

Set your goals for the cycle

Create goals with a title, description, and target date. Keep them updated as work progresses — your manager can see them and will use them as context when writing your review.

3

Give & receive feedback

Respond to peer-review requests at your own pace using save-draft. Request feedback from colleagues you've worked closely with. Managers write assessments and share them directly in 1:1s.

4

Submit expenses, close tickets

After every trip or project, photograph your receipts and submit expenses in seconds — the app auto-fills fields from Portuguese invoice QR codes and validates the company NIF. Raise an IT or People Ops ticket any time you need support.
